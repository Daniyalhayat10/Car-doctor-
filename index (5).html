Who We Are

A Garage You Can
Actually Trust

Thames Side Car Doctor has been on River Road in Barking for over 15 years. We started out as a small tyre shop. Over time word spread and we grew into a full-service garage that now looks after thousands of private cars, PCO vehicles and small fleets across East London.

Our founder Hammad has always run things on one simple rule: be straight with people. No made-up faults, no charging for parts that do not need replacing. If your car does not need a job done, we will tell you. That is why so many of our customers have been coming back for years and send their family and friends to us.
